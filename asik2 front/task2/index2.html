About Adolf Hitler

Adolf Hitler (20 April 1889 – 30 April 1945) was an Austrian-born German politician who was the dictator of Germany during the Nazi period from 1933 until his suicide in 1945. He rose to power as the leader of the Nazi Party.

Achievements

  • Joined the German Workers' Party (later NSDAP) and became Chancellor in 1933
  • Violated the Treaty of Versailles, pursued territorial expansion
  • Orchestrated the Holocaust, resulting in the systemic murder of six million Jews and millions of others
  • Devastating human cost, world war, and the eventual downfall of Nazi Germany in 1945
